About Us

Brooklyn College Programming Club is a platform where any students who is interested in coding or programming, get together to work collaborately and learn amazing things from each others. Through that, we get an opportunity to socialize, make friends, have a diverting campus life, make timeless treasures of memories. Besides that, we also get academic help and advisement from each other. Leaders and members of this family have taken the time to know more about new softwares, frameworks and tools, practice challenging projects in a collaborative environment which makes them eager to learn about leadership, responsibilities, time management, event management, networking, public speaking, and so on. By joining our club, you won't lose anything instead you will learn a lot.

What we do

We arrange events like the "Hackathon" or "CUNY codes" that help us boost our technical skills. You can also get help online from our website anytime just by creating a thread and explain your issue. Other members will try to reply with a solution.
